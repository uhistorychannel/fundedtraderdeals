What is a prop firm?

A proprietary trading firm provides capital to traders. You trade their money, keep most profits, and they take on the risk. First you pass an evaluation/challenge to prove your skills.

Which prop firm is best for beginners?

For forex: FundedNext offers lower prices and relaxed rules. For futures: Apex has the most aggressive discounts (80% OFF) making it affordable to start.

Forex vs Futures prop firms?

Forex firms (FTMO, FundedNext) offer CFDs on currencies, indices, commodities. Futures firms (Apex, Topstep) trade actual futures contracts on CME, typically with better profit splits.
